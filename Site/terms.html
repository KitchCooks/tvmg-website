1. Scope of service

TVMG produces presentation/slide-based narrated videos only. Our service does not include live filming, custom animation, stock-footage editing, or bespoke video work of any kind. Any request falling outside presentation/slide-based narrated video will be declined or quoted separately as a distinct engagement.

2. Client-supplied content

The client provides all source content via the brief, including text, data, structure, and any logos or images to be featured. The client is solely responsible for the accuracy, legality, and completeness of all supplied content. TVMG produces the video based on the content as supplied and is not responsible for verifying its factual accuracy.

3. Production technology

TVMG makes use of advanced artificial intelligence tools to deliver the content, so that the voice can be tailored to your liking and produced in an accent of your choice.

4. Languages

For any language other than English or Afrikaans, it is the client's responsibility to have a fluent or native speaker review and approve the language version before final acceptance. TVMG is not liable for language nuance or accuracy errors in any version the client has not had reviewed.

5. Revisions

Three (3) revision rounds are included with every standalone video. Additional revision rounds are charged at R3,000 per round thereafter. A revision means changes to supplied content or narration. It does not include re-scoping the video (for example, changing the topic, length tier, or structure of the project), which is quoted separately.

6. Turnaround

Stated turnaround windows are estimates and begin once the brief and payment are received. Urgent delivery ahead of the standard window can be accommodated and is quoted as a premium.

7. Payment & refund

Standalone videos

Standalone videos are payable 100% upfront before production begins. A 7-day satisfaction guarantee applies: if, after the three (3) included revision rounds, the client is genuinely unhappy with the final video, they may request a full refund within 7 days of final delivery. This is a satisfaction (quality) guarantee, not a change-of-mind or "no longer needed" refund.

High-ticket projects (onboarding systems, enterprise/government libraries)

High-ticket projects require a 70% deposit upfront before production begins, with the 30% balance due on or before final delivery. Bespoke terms may apply per project. The 7-day standalone refund above does not apply to high-ticket projects.

Retainers

Retainers are billed monthly in advance.

In all cases, the final deliverable is released only on full payment.
